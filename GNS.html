GNS 203 PRACTICAL

PRODUCTION OF LIQUID SOAP

The Ingeredents needed for the production of liquid soap goes thus;
  1. Sodium Laurate Sulphate (SLC) Texapon
  2. Sulphoni Acid
  3. Caustic Soda
  4. Soda ash
  5. Sodium tripolyphosphate(STPP)
  6. Formalin and Texapon
  7. Colorant and Perfume

MATERIALS NEEDED

  • A big balf
  • Big turning stick
  • Hand gloves
  • Small turning stick
  • Nose mask
  • Eye google
  • Small buckets
  • 1 ragolis container
  • Any other things needed for safety precaution

PRE-PRODUCTION PROCESS

  • Mix sulphonic acid and texapon together in a big bucket not adding water
  • Measure 20liters of water into the balf
  • Divide the water into two equal half, keep one aside and soak chemial foam into the other half
  • Mix the Caustic soda with 1litre of water. Stir until it dissolves.
  • Mix the STPP with 1litre of water.
  • Dissolve the colour in a small water inside the ragolis container.

PROPER PRODUCTION

  • Put Natrosol inside 10litre of water(the 10litre half set aside).
  • Add the mixed Sulphonic and Texapon.
  • Add the caustic Soda.
  • Add the Soda ash, but this time little by little.
    Note:-As it gets thicken, add from the remaining water you used for soaking.
  • Add STPP then add SLS
  • Add foaming booster
  • Add your perfume and lastly colour
